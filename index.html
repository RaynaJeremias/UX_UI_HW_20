Government Website
Redesign
Case Study

Rayna Jeremias & Talia Dobrec
UX/UI Designers

Project Duration- 2 weeks


Overview
Project Brief-
Redesign a government website based on a comprehensive
user analysis and heuristics evaluation.
The Problem-
Arizona.gov needed a major overhaul, from content to continuity.
The Goal-
To create an esthetically pleasing site that has excellent usability.

  • Ideation

Research

My partner and I began our project by doing a heuristic evaluation of the current website. We created a short user test and had people try to navigate the site. The website felt cold and unwelcoming, with no content other than a single, large image of Coronavirus. Navigation had many flaws, including clicking the tab you wanted and landing on another option page, which was frustrating to users. Another issue was the overwhelming amount of external links.

Pain Points
•Navigation confusion
•Overwhelming amount of external links
•Not a lot of content

My partner used Google Analytics to find key data on our site.
It was surprising to see how high the site bounce rate was- 56%
